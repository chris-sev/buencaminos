About Us

Mikki Buencamino

This guy is unbelievable. He makes me laugh and knows how to handle my obnoxious ways. Although he has a habit of cleaning his glasses and phone every morning and every night, I still find it quite fascinating and him, quite amazing. I've known Mikki for a really long time and he can be tough to handle at times. But he cares. He has the warmest heart and love for the people he cares for. The sacrifices he has made for me is beyond words. When he looks at me, I know his love is unconditional. Love is feeling weightless of burden because you don't have to do it alone.
