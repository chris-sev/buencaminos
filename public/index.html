About Us

Mikki Buencamino

"This guy is unbelievable. He makes me laugh and knows how to handle my obnoxious ways. Although he has a habit of cleaning his glasses and phone every morning and every night, I still find it quite fascinating and him, quite amazing. I've known Mikki for a really long time and he can be tough to handle at times. But he cares. He has the warmest heart and love for the people he lets into his life. The sacrifices he has made for me is beyond words. He has made me nothing but strong and confident. I feel my best when he's around. When he looks at me, I know his love is unconditional. Love is feeling weightless of burden because you don't have to do it alone." ♥ Lynne

Lynne Mendoza

"Lynne is the reason why I've come to realize that love heals everything. I was broken when she first met me. She broke the wall that I built and taught me to love again. I'm the man I am today because of her. She's my number one cheerleader who encouraged me to chase my dreams. I'm a very angry and stressful person. She helped me be the best person that I can be and teaching me that life is something to be thankful for and not get mad at. She's the happiest person I know. That's one of the million things I love about her. She can always turn my frown into a smile. I'm very lucky to have found a person who can handle me in such a way that makes me a better person." - Mikki
